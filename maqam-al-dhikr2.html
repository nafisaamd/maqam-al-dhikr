About Maqam al-Dhikr
مقام الذكر
A personal Islamic dhikr companion
Version 1.0
What is this app?

Maqam al-Dhikr is a personal dhikr and dua companion designed to support your daily worship — from morning and evening adhkar to a private du'a journal, salah guidance, and the 99 Names of Allah.

Everything runs locally on your device. Your du'a journal is private and never leaves your phone.

Sources & Authenticity

All adhkar, duas, and hadith references in this app are drawn from authenticated primary sources:

  • Hisnul Muslim (Fortress of the Muslim) — Sheikh Said al-Qahtani
  • Sahih al-Bukhari and Sahih Muslim
  • Sunan Abu Dawud, Tirmidhi, and Ibn Majah
  • Riyad al-Salihin — Imam al-Nawawi

Minor details in fiqh (such as exact madhab rulings on salah steps) may differ. When in doubt, consult a scholar.

How to use
  • Tasbeeh — Tap the ring or press Space to count. Select your dhikr from the cards below.
  • Morning & Evening — Tap a card to count each recitation. Tap a completed card to reset it.
  • Salah Companion — Tap any step to expand the Arabic, transliteration, and meaning.
  • Du'a Journal — Write personal duas, tag them, and mark them as answered when Allah responds.
  • 99 Names — A new name is featured each day. Search or filter by theme.
Save to Home Screen

To use this app offline and add it to your phone's home screen:

  1. Open this page in Safari on iPhone
  2. Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow)
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add — it will appear as an app on your home screen
